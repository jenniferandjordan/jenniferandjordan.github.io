The Story

About six years ago, Jennifer and Jordan were introduced by a mutual friend (or friends, depending on who you ask). They went from complete strangers, to friends of friends, to inseparable college sweethearts. This is their story...

September 2005

He packs his bags and leaves Tulsa, Oklahoma. Tufts University, here we come…

September 2005

She moves cross country, trading her sandals for winter boots. Tufts University was calling…

Three years later, the couple’s paths have not yet crossed…but they came close on many occasions:

  • They were enrolled in the same Introduction to Yiddish Culture class freshmen year. Jordan went to every class, while Jenifer’s attendance was more sporadic.
  • They pledged Sister/Brother Greek organizations: Jennifer with Alpha Phi and Jordan with AEPi.
  • They lived in the same area of campus, ate in the same dining halls, and attended the same parties…fate would not have them meet.

September 2008

She’s on the hunt to find “nice Jewish boy”. She asks her friend, Kenny Fifer, for some suggestions.

September 2008

His friend and Sophomore-year roommate (and Groomsmen), Kenny Fifer, suggests he gets to know Jennifer Gal. Kenny’s got a good feeling about this one.

During a night out with friends in Harvard Square, Jennifer and Jordan finally speak for the first time. They bond over food, particularly sushi. Inspiration strikes and he (sort of*) asks Jennifer out on a first date of sushi making.

*Note: Jordan was unclear if he actually asked Jennifer out. So he did what anyone would do in his position. He called to confirm their dinner, an act which Jennifer still makes fun of to this day.

Late September 2008

He frantically shops at Whole Foods with guidance from his friend and Senior-year roommate (and Groomsmen), Benjie Moll. He anxiously lugs home a bag full of raw fish, seaweed, vegetables, rice and Japanese beer. Despite convincing Jennifer that he is basically a professional sushi chef, this is the first time he’s actually made real sushi. He spends the next several hours meticulously cleaning every inch of the apartment, crafting the perfect iTunes playlist, prepping the kitchen…and drinking alone to settle his nerves.

Late September 2008

She throws on jeans and a top for what is expected to be casual dinner at Jordan’s apartment.

The sushi may have been average, but the date was a success! A first date leads to a second date – this time Jordan was a little less ambitious with the preparations, but still called Jennifer to confirm – which leads to a third date, which we all know leads to…a fourth date (get your mind out of the gutter).

July 2009

He moves to New York City to start his new job and experience one of the most exciting cities in the world (..and instead work tirelessly for JPMorgan).

September 2009

She plans a 5-day trip to New York City to search for jobs, lands a job on Day 1 of the trip, and starts the following day. A box of clothes is overnighted cross-country.

After 2 years together in New York City, it’s time for Jennifer to steal Jordan away to LA LA Land.

July 2011

She introduces him to LA Sushi, the beach, a life without snow, and massages covered by medical insurance.

July 2011

MIND BLOWN.

Two years later, on November 23, 2013, Jordan proposes to Jennifer in the SLS Hotel in Los Angeles, California.
